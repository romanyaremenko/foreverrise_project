Quick start

to have a passive income

To activate this simple type of earnings, you need to connect with your Metamask / Trust Wallet and have at least 0.3 BNB worth of Foreverise tokens on your wallet. As soon as your wallet is connected, you can start watching an endless feed of commercials, going on one by one, like the advertisements in a movie theater. This is NOT the most fun and exciting leisure activity but every second of your USDT balance increasing will for sure brighten it up!

More phones = More money

You can multiply your earnings the more phones you have (not lower than Android 5 Lollipop) or a Used iPhone (not lower than iOS 10.3), connect it to the charger, enter the application and leave it working for a month without closing the application.
